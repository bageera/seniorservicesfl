Why Choose Senior Support Servicing?

We are an in-home care agency that provides superior services that allow seniors to remain safely in their own homes while receiving top-of-the-line personalized care that fits your family's needs. We provide a full continuum of care from skilled nursing to companion.

Our main focus is to help seniors regain their confidence and independence after illness or injury, to help them spend their golden years soaking up the joys and happiness of life.

We can help you or your loved one in the comfort of your own home

Our health care professionals will ensure that your loved one receives the specialized and planned care they need in the comfort of their homes. We help your loved one regain independence after illness or injury, regain strength, learn how to self-manage conditions and maintain a safe environment. We have an RN on call 24/7 for your family's peace of mind. These are just a few of the areas Senior Support Servicing can assist you with:

  • Dementia
  • Parkinson's disease
  • Stroke recovery
  • Chronic pain
  • Skilled Nursing
  • Physical Therapy
  • Occupational Therapy
  • Speech Therapy
  • Medical Social Services
  • Home Health Aide and Certified Nursing Assistant
  • DME
  • Private Duty
