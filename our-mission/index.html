MISSION

Senior Support Servicing's Mission is to provide superior skilled and unskilled services throughout the community we serve. We will provide superior services by recruiting compassionate and experienced clinicians who are culturally diverse and are oriented to our inclusive mindset throughout our entire servicing population.

GOALS

Our goal is to be the best Senior Support agency in the communities we serve by exceeding the needs and expectations of all patients, physicians, clinicians, and payers by continuously improving the quality of our services.

Our Objectives

  • Prevent admission into the hospital setting.
  • Increase patient/clinicians satisfaction with care, by designing a Plan of Care that fits YOU.
  • Explain your plan completely in person and in writing.
  • Ease your transition to a maximum functional level at home.
